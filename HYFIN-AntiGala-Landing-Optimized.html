About the Event

Celebrate Black Excellence at the Cookout of the Year

The HYFIN Anti-Gala is an evening that blends the energy of a family-style cookout, the nostalgia of the 80s, and the power of cultural storytelling.

Guests will experience a one-of-a-kind atmosphere featuring DJs spinning classic sounds of the decade, onsite activations, exclusive vinyl packages, and an evening that celebrates Black Milwaukee with one of the best parties of the year.

The evening honors four distinguished awardees whose work embodies HYFIN’s pillars: Music & Culture, Entrepreneurship, Youth Education, and Storytelling.
